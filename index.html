"Kreupasanam Amma — the Blessed Mother who holds the clock close to her heart, reminding us that every moment belongs to God."

Kreupasanam is a sacred Marian shrine of deep spiritual significance, where countless souls have experienced the tender mercy and grace of Our Lady. The image of Amma holding a clock to her heart is a powerful reminder that time itself is a gift, and that every hour is an opportunity to draw closer to God through her intercession.

Sacred Words

A Prayer to Kreupasanam Amma

O Blessed Kreupasanam Amma,
Mother of Grace and Mercy,
you who hold time in your sacred heart —
intercede for us before your Son,
that in every moment of our lives
we may know your love and protection.

Heal our wounds, console our sorrows,
and guide our steps toward eternal light.

Amen. ✦
